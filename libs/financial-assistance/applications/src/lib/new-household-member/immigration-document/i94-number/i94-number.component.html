• When entering the I-94 Number, only include 9 numbers followed by a letter or a number in the 10th position and a number in the 11th position. Do not enter any other characters or spaces.
  • You must enter exactly 11 characters into the I-94 field.
  • How to find the I-94 Number: The I-94 number is also called the admissions number. It is an 11 character sequence found printed on Arrival/Departure records (For I-94 or Form I-94A.) It can also be found on the form I-9.
