Is this person a member of an American Indian or Alaska Native Tribe?

American Indians, members of federally recognized Tribes, Alaska Native Claims Settlement Act (ANCSA) corporation shareholders, and those eligible for services from the Indian Health Service, Tribal Program, or Urban Indian Health Program are eligible for enhanced savings, benefits and protections through DC Health Link. Apply for financial assistance prior to enrolling in a health plan to determine eligibility for the savings, benefits and protections available.
