IRS Form 8962

To calculate your premium tax credit, you'll need your second lowest cost Silver plan (SLCSP) premiums to fill out IRS Form 8962.

Your SLCSP premium can be found on your Form 1095-A, but it may be missing or incorrect. We'll ask a few questions on the next screens and give you the correct SLCSP premium.
